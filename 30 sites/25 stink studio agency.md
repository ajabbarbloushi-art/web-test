# Site 25: Stink Studios Agency

## Overview
- **URL**: [https://www.stinkstudios.com/](https://www.stinkstudios.com/)
- **Award**: Over 25 SOTD winners, SOTM, and numerous other industry awards.
- **Agency**: Stink Studios
- **Core Concept**: A global creative studio that combines "thinking" and "making" under one roof. Their site is a masterclass in modern, high-performance web development and clean, brand-forward design that caters to massive global clients like Google, Spotify, and Nike.

## Design & UI/UX
- **Visual Style**: Clean, modern, and high-contrast. The site uses a lot of bold typography, large-scale imagery, and a modular grid system that feels both organized and dynamic.
- **Brand-Forward Content**: The design is secondary to the work. Large video reels and project case studies are the focus, demonstrating their capabilities across advertising, digital, and branding.
- **Sophisticated Dark Mode**: The site features a beautifully executed dark mode (with a light mode toggle) that uses subtle grays and high-contrast accents to maintain readability and impact.
- **Global Editorial Feel**: The layout feels like a high-end digital magazine, with large headlines and clear, concise project descriptions.

## Animation & Interaction
- **High-Performance Video**: Seamless integration of high-quality video backgrounds and reels that load quickly and play smoothly without impacting site performance.
- **Subtle, Purposeful Motion**: Animations are used to guide the user's eye and provide feedback, rather than just for show. Think smooth scroll reveals, elegant hover states, and seamless page transitions.
- **Modular Interaction**: The grid elements often have subtle hover effects that reveal more information or trigger video playback, encouraging exploration.
- **Responsive Precision**: The site is perfectly optimized for all devices, with a mobile experience that feels just as premium as the desktop version.

## Technical Stack & Tools
- **Core Engine**: **Next.js** for a high-performance, SEO-friendly React application.
- **Animation**: **Framer Motion** and **GSAP** for the sophisticated UI animations and transitions.
- **Content Management**: Headless CMS architecture to manage their vast global portfolio.
- **Video Optimization**: Advanced video compression and streaming techniques to ensure high-quality playback across all network speeds.

## Key Inspiration Points
1. **The Power of the Reel**: A high-quality showreel is the best way to immediately communicate your studio's quality and range.
2. **Performance is a Design Feature**: An award-winning site must be fast. Technical optimization (like using Next.js) is as important as the visual design.
3. **Cater to the "Big Dogs"**: Use a clean, professional, and confident design language that appeals to Fortune 500 clients.
4. **Modular Flexibility**: Build a design system that is flexible enough to showcase a wide variety of work while maintaining a consistent brand identity.
